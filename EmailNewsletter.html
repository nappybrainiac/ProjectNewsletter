Welcome back fellow BucBucinions!

As we hurtle towards the great unknown, our tasks aboard this ship consume us so much, we forget what it means to have normal and regular human interaction. To this end, we have started a venture that is an oldie but a goodie.

At the end of the 20th Century when the internet was still in its infancy, human beings would blog (short for web log). They created a space on the internet where they shared stories, views, and resources, connecting people to one another regarless of their location on Earth.

In the 21st Century microblogging was introduced. Rather than writing lengthy articles, people shared tiny snippets to a site, where those who were interested in what they had to say, would follow them. Sometimes a person would end up with up to one million followers, who received a notice on a handheld device when a new post was created.
